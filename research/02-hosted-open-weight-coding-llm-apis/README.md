## One-sentence takeaway

For a cheap, safe-enough one-week trial, start with **OpenRouter with ZDR/provider filtering plus a low spend cap**, pin or prefer **DeepInfra/Fireworks/Together** endpoints, and compare **Kimi K2.6**, **Qwen3-Coder 480B**, **DeepSeek V4/3.1**, and **Llama 4 Maverick** against your real Opus/GPT-5.5 baseline using the same coding-agent harness, validation commands, and cost logs.

## Current confidence

High confidence on published pricing/context and privacy controls as of 2026-05-07; medium confidence on relative coding quality because vendor and benchmark claims move quickly; low-to-medium confidence on provider reliability unless you measure your own latency, tool-call failures, and outage behavior during the week.
