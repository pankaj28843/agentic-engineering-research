developments from 1-22 July 2026 for a practitioner returning from a long break.
July 22 is a partial-day edition at the recorded evidence cutoff.
It is a dated newspaper, not a list of everything that received attention.

Start with the [reader's edition](guide/00-README.md). The one-page period map
provides the fast route; the daily chapters preserve the chronology and link to

## Scope

The reporting window begins 1 July 2026 00:00 and extends through the partial
22 July edition's `2026-07-22T21:44:10+02:00` evidence cutoff in
`Europe/Copenhagen`. Topics include
model and tool releases, agent engineering, RAG, model economics, production
failures, evaluation, security, policy with engineering consequences, and
evidence about the economics or quality of AI-generated work.

Attention is not selection evidence. Items are ranked by engineering
consequence, catch-up dependency, material novelty or correction, durability,
and breadth. Hacker News and social discussion qualify interpretation and help
locate missing evidence; they do not prove an event by themselves.
