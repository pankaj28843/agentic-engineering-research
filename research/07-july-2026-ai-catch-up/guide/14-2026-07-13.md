## What else mattered

<article markdown="1">

### Apple's SpeechAnalyzer beats Whisper Small in a bounded test

Inscribe ran 5,559 LibriSpeech utterances on an M2 Pro and released the transcripts. It reported SpeechAnalyzer word-error rates of 2.12% on clean speech and 4.56% on noisy speech, versus 3.74% and 7.95% for Whisper Small, while completing inference about three times faster. The artifact is reproducible enough to inform an Apple-platform decision, but it tests English audiobook speech and does not include newer Parakeet, Nemotron, Voxtral, or Cohere comparators.

<div class="impact" markdown="1">

**Builder impact:** For English transcription on Apple hardware, benchmark SpeechAnalyzer locally; do not infer multilingual or frontier-model superiority from this dataset.

</div>
